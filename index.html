About Hawla Riza - Maldivian Poet

Hawla Riza is a pioneering Maldivian poet and author. Her work focuses on mental wellness, cultural identity, hope, healing, and the profound connection between people and the islands of the Maldives. As the author of "Heal in Paradise," Hawla has created the first Maldivian literary souvenir, allowing readers worldwide to experience the healing essence of island life through poetry.

Poetry Themes: Hope, Healing, and Island Life

Heal in Paradise explores universal themes of hope and healing through the unique lens of Maldivian island life. The poetry collection addresses mental wellness, emotional healing, cultural identity, environmental connection, and the transformative power of nature. Each poem serves as a bridge between the reader and the healing energy of the Maldives.
